# Recomended Combinations

The best part about this plugin is that every gamemode is fully compatible with every other gamemode, and modifiers can be layered on top of any of them. That said, it's definitely true that some combinations are better than others. Here, I will provide a list of what (from my and my tester's experience) were the most enjoyable combinations.

Remember that gamemodes are enabled with `/mg EnableGamemode <name>` and modifiers with `/mg EnableModifier <name>`.

**DimensionSwap + BlockShuffle:**
<br>
This works particularly well because exploring new dimensions exposes you to a lot of block types and allows players who are not as good at pvp to catchup by focusing on finding the right blocks.

**Deathmatch + Randomizer** *(gamemode + modifier)*
<br>
One of my favorite combinations because oftentimes the items that players manage to obtain and are forced to fight with are random and non-meta, which results in way more interesting fights.

**DeathSwap + WouldYouRather:**
<br>
This is a great combination because as the game goes on, players will have more ways to evade death via their WouldYouRather abilities, so players need to get more creative on how to kill people over time.

**DeathSwap + DimensionSwap + WouldYouRather + BlockShuffle, plus the Randomizer modifier:**
<br>
Very chaotic, but also very fun. Players will have a million different things they have to manage, but overall it's an awesome experience, especially with a large group.
